About ISOM Data Lab
@ Perdue School of Business

A free, student-centered analytics support community — built within Salisbury University's Perdue School of Business to help every ISOM student thrive with data.

Our Mission

We believe no Sea Gull student should be stuck on a coding problem alone.

🌱

Accessible Learning

Free coding support for every Perdue student, regardless of background or experience level.

🔗

Community Connections

Building lasting relationships among Sea Gulls — students, seniors, and faculty — through shared knowledge.

📊

Analytics Excellence

Elevating data literacy across the Perdue School of Business — one session at a time.
